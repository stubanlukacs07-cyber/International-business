Seminar 02
◆ 05 March 2026

Globalisation — pros & cons

What is globalisation and why does it happen? Benefits and risks for host countries, using McDonald’s in Ireland, China’s globalisation video, South Korea’s “infant industry” policy of 1963, and India’s 2025 steel safeguard tariff as anchoring cases.

Global supply chains FDI Infant industry Strategic industries Tariffs
